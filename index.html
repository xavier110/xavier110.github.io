Why choose minimalOS?

A lightweight modification of Windows, designed for anyone alike who desires a trusted & performant experience without sacrificing functionality.

  • Enhanced privacy: Reduces Windows telemetry & data collection by disabling services.
  • Better performance By removing useless features packed in stock Windows, minimalOS can give more performances, even on low-end PCs.
  • Better user experience: minimalOS has a few minor tweaks that make the UX better.
